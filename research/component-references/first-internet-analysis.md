## Patterns to Extract (Inferred from Agency Best Practices)

### Navigation Pattern (Premium Agency Standard)
- Sticky header with transparent-to-solid transition on scroll
- Logo left, nav links center/right, CTA button far right
- Hamburger menu on mobile with full-screen or slide-in panel
- Likely includes mega-menu for service categories

### Hero Pattern
- Full-width hero with bold headline
- Benefit-driven copy (not feature-driven)
- Strong primary CTA + secondary action
- Background: likely image/video or subtle animation

### Service Presentation
- Individual service pages (not just cards on homepage)
- Process/methodology sections showing how they work
- Each service page likely has its own CTA

### Trust/Social Proof
- Awards page (dedicated credibility page)
- Case studies with results
- Client logos
- Testimonials with names + companies

### Content Strategy
- Active blog covering industry trends
- Educational content positioning expertise
- Each blog post likely has CTAs to services

## Key Takeaways for BND Labs

1. **Dedicated service pages** per offering (Growth Starter, Retainer, AI Engine each get their own page/section)
2. **Awards/credibility page** — BND Labs should have a trust/results section
3. **Industry vertical pages** — healthcare, hospitality, education landing pages
4. **Bold typography trend** aligns with Plus Jakarta Sans display font choice
5. **Portfolio/Work page** with case studies is critical for conversion
6. **Blog as SEO engine** — active content hub driving organic traffic
7. **Benefit-driven copy** throughout (not feature lists)
8. **Process/methodology section** — shows how BND Labs works (systems approach)
