## Design Context

### Users
Established Zambian business owners in healthcare, hospitality, and education sectors. They're browsing during work hours on phones and laptops, likely frustrated by unreliable freelancers, and looking for a professional digital partner who can deliver predictable lead generation. They want systems, not one-off projects.

### Brand Personality
Confident, systematic, warm — trusted advisor energy. Professional authority with approachable warmth. Not cold or corporate, but not casual either.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Emotional goal**: Relief + confidence — "Finally, someone who gets it"
- **Theme**: Both light and dark (system preference)
- **Visual tone**: Professional, structured, warm — should feel like a well-run consultancy, not a flashy startup
- **Anti-patterns**: Generic agency templates, overly techy/cold aesthetics, freelancer vibes
- **Color palette**: Deep Purple (#2D1B69) primary, Burnt Orange (#b85a35) accent, with molecular accent nodes (cyan #6dd5ea, purple #b06cc8, orange #e07848, yellow #ecd04a)
- **Fonts**: Source Sans 3 (body), Epilogue (display), Geist Mono (code)

### Design Principles
1. **Systems over flash** — Every visual element should reinforce the "we build reliable systems" message
2. **Warm authority** — Professional enough to trust with your business, warm enough to feel like a partner
3. **Clarity first** — Business owners are busy; every section should communicate value in seconds
4. **Zambian context** — Design for the local market; respect the audience's sophistication without being patronizing
5. **Progressive trust** — Build confidence through the page flow: problem → solution → proof → action
